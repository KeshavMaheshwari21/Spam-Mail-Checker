The primary goal of an email classification model is to distinguish between legitimate emails (ham) and unsolicited or potentially harmful emails (spam). This helps users manage their inboxes more effectively and avoid potential security risks.

In summary, an email classification model plays a crucial role in automating the sorting of emails into ham or spam categories. By leveraging machine learning techniques and continuously adapting to new patterns, these models help users maintain a cleaner and more secure inbox.
